George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is a public college with three full campuses located in downtown Toronto (St.James Campus, Casa Loma Campus and Waterfront Campus). As a George Brown student, student will be able to access to the thriving social, recreational activities and personal growth experiences that big city Toronto have to offer. At George Brown College, students have a wide variety of programs to choose from. These programs give you the skills you need to succeed in the modern workplace. Like many other colleges in Ontario, GBC was chartered in 1966 by the government of Ontario and opened the next year.
